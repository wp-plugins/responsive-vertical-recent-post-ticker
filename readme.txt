Displays recent posts as vertical ticker.

== Description ==

Displays recent posts as vertical ticker in reponsive mode.There are various options in admin section to set speed,direction,no.of posts to show and interval.

1. Use the shortcode **<code>[vertical_post_ticker]</code>**code in your posts or pages.

1. Use this **<code><?php echo do_shortcode(['vertical_post_ticker]'); ?></code>**in your templates directly.

== Installation ==


1. Upload `wp-vertical-post-ticker` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Use the shortcode `[vertical_post_ticker]` in your posts or pages.
1. Use this `<?php echo do_shortcode(['vertical_post_ticker]'); ?>` in your templates directly.

**Admin Settings**

After Activate the plugin Go to Settings-->Vertical Ticker Settings
